Our leadership/management

We are goverened by members,through the Annual General Assembly,The Board of Directors and the Secretariat.


The Annual General Assembly is the supreme organ of the organization.It comprises all the organization's members.All bodies established under its authority are surbodinate and accountbale to the General Assembly.


The second organ is the Board of Directors,which provides regular guidance to the work of the organization;they are responsible for the policy and administration of general and business matters of the organization.The Board comprises a minimum of five and a maximum of seven members who meet periodically.They appoint the Executive Director who is secretary to the Board and is responsible for the day to day management of the organization.He/she is assisted by a management team.
Members hold office for a term of not more than 3 years and are eligible for reappointment.


The implementation of the day to day functions of the organization is vested in the Secretariat,which is led by the Executive Director, who reports to the Board of Directors.The supporting staff for the Secretariat are appointed and/or recruited by the Executive Director on a contractual basis,with their contracts subject to renewal upon successful performance.


Our organization's executive management and administration is headed by the Managing Director.It involves the departments of:

  • Human Resources
  • Finance
  • Information Technology
  • Public Relations
  • Livelihood
  • Advocacy
