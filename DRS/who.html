Who we are

DISABILITY RELIEF SERVICES(DRS) TANZANIA is a Non-Government Organization.Established in 2008 and legally registered on 16th October 2008 (registration no.OONGO/00002597),we deal with improvement of socio-economic conditions in vulnerable,marginalized and deprived communities in refugee camps and host communities in Tanzania.


We have a mandate to operate all over Tanzania and in Nyarugusu Refugee Camp with a permit from the Ministry of Home Affairs.


Our core activities are:

  • Social Protection Interventions for People With Special Needs(PWSNs) and adolescent girls and young women(AGYW)
  • Advocacy and promotion for human rights
  • Actions to end Violence Against Women and Children(VAWC)
  • Social economic empowerment
  • Environment
  • Zero Human Trafficking
  • Health


Our principles

Our focus anchors towards ensuring that our beneficiaries understand and confidently proclaim their indivdual and group rights.We also aim to be actively involved in
all community development endeavors,ranging from family economic gains to social and political interactions.Hence why we practise effective strategic and inclusive empowerment initiatives,so as to ensure right operational results.We do all this through our expertise and long experience in provision of structured and tailored
human capital development programs as well as creation of life based and sustainable varied projects.


Finally,we assist beneficiaries to unleash their hidden potentials and become valuable contributors to both their own living and to the society's development at large.We cherish being among the active players within the locations we reach,in promoting and valuing active citizenship,providing support and working in partnership with our target population and the communities they live in;a factor envisaged within our organization's core theme of "Equity for life".


Our Vision

To have a society where equity,social justice and rights of Persons with Special Needs(PSNs) within stipulated baskets are guaranteed.


Our Mission

To transform PSNs to be dynamic,professional,efficient and effective in the realization of their rights and justice and to also contribute towards ensuring equal existence and improved living status to all.We exist towards improved livelihood and well being to all citizens.


Our core values

  • Respect and vulnerability
  • Gender inclusion
  • Trust and honesty
  • Creativity and innovation
  • Partnership and collaboration
  • Equity and diversity
  • Transparency and accountability
