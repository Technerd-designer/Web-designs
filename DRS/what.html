What we do

By and large,and with the urge to add real value to the community in broader sense,DRS-Tanzania focuses towards five ultimate sustainable results,through effective service provisions.


The first result focus is supporting Persons with Special Needs(PSNs) individually and within set groups to enable them improve their living status and within their
entire llvelihood journey.This includes making beneficiaries understand themselves as people responsible for their own living.


The second result-based focus is ensuring fully inclusive existence,understanding and involvement in all societal engagements.


The third focus is on triggering prosperity and development advancement through formalized functional groups.


Our fourth focus is supporting and facilitating towards strengthening the organizational capacity of active Community Based Organizations(CBOs) dealing to any basket of esteemed beneficiaries.This is done through tailored training initiatives and ensuring existence of effective leadership and good governance practices for the benefit
of the beneficiaries they serve.


The fifth and final focus is propagating,through effective facilitations and sensitization programs,to the existence of peace and harmony among beneficiaries as specific groups and also to the entire society they live in.Essence of this is to ensure happy and advantageous living status to all citizens.


Specific functions

Most of the projects,programs and activities undertaken by our organization are generally associated with efforts in People with Special Needs programs. Such projects,programs and activities are:

The social protection program focuses on providing support to people with special needs and adolescent girls and young women.It provides primary and secondary school AGYW from poor households with the necessary resources to continue their education.Additionally,it also provides support to out of school vulnerable AGYW aged 15-24,helping to prevent them from falling into poverty and becoming vulnerable to exploitation.

The goal of this program is to create a safer and more just world for women and children by addressing and ending violence against them.It provides support to victims of violence,including access to medical care,counseling and legal assistanceAdditionally,the program works to raise awareness about the issue of violence against women and children and to engage communities in the fight to end it.

This program aims to provide people with the opportunities and resources they need to achieve sustainable growth and success.This may include training programs,access to finance and markets and other support to help individuals start and grow their businesses.Additionally, the program may focus on empowering women and other marginalized groups,helping to reduce inequality and create a more inclusive and equitable society.

This program works to protect the vulnerable and ensure their rights are respected.This includes providing education and awareness-raising activities,
as well as providing support to victims of trafficking.The program may also work to strengthen the legal and regulatory framework around human trafficking and to engage the community in the fight against it.

The health program focuses on improving access to quality healthcare for all.This may include providing medical services and support to underserved communities,improving health facilities and increasing awareness about important health issues.The program may also work to strengthen the healthcare system,making it more effective and efficient and to ensure that everyone has access to the care they need.

The education program aims to transform lives through learning and development.This may include providing access to quality education for people who are underserved,supporting teacher training and development and improving educational facilities.Additionally,the program may work to raise awareness about the importance of education and to engage the communities in the fight to ensure that everyone has access to the education the need.

Our Strategic Research Department undertakes policy research and analysis to increase stakeholders' knowledge through generation of research evidence,which forms a major input to economic,social and other policy-making processes in the country.Both demand and supply-driven research is planned and implemented,with the intention to respond to the socio-economic changes and advance knowledge in key areas of national,regional or global importance.
The research agenda is therefore shaped by the evolving economic policies,the dynamics of political governance and the emerging social economic challenges emanating from the changing economic environment in Tanzania,the EAC and worldwide.The research is also dictated by emerging global issues that have increased the need for research based evidence.
Our annual plans are geared towards carrying out rigorous and policy oriented research that generates new insights and options for promoting policies that facilitate mineral-related inclusive growth.
